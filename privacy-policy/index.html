Privacy Policy

Digital Asset Vault GB Content Manager
Last updated: 18 May 2026

Digital Asset Vault GB respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our website and connected content tools are used and what information they may access.

Who We Are

Digital Asset Vault GB creates digital templates, Excel workbooks, business bundles and planning tools for small businesses, creators and individuals.

Our connected content tools may be used by Digital Asset Vault GB to manage our own business content, including creating pins, organising boards and reviewing content performance.

Information We Access

When connected to third-party platforms such as Pinterest, our app may access information permitted by the API scopes approved during authorisation, such as account details, boards, pins and basic reporting data for our own business account.

If you submit your email address through this website, we may store your email address and source page so we can send relevant updates about Digital Asset Vault GB products.

How We Use Information

  • To create and publish content for Digital Asset Vault GB products
  • To organise content into relevant boards or campaigns
  • To review performance and improve our marketing workflow
  • To send product updates when someone chooses to join our list

We do not sell personal information.

Data Sharing

We do not share account data with third parties except where required to operate the website/app, comply with the law, or use the relevant platform API services.

Data Storage

Access tokens and API credentials are stored securely and used only for authorised account management. Website leads may be stored in our Supabase database or email platform when configured.

Data Retention

We retain app-related access only for as long as needed to manage Digital Asset Vault GB content. Access can be revoked at any time through the relevant third-party account settings.

User Choices

If you are the account owner, you can revoke app access through your platform account settings. If you joined our email list, you can unsubscribe or request removal.

Children’s Privacy

This website and app are not directed at children and do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Digital Asset Vault GB via our Etsy shop: https://www.etsy.com/shop/DigitalAssetVaultGB
