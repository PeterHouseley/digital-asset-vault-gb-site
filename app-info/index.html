Digital Asset Vault GB Content Manager

This page explains the intended use of the Digital Asset Vault GB Content Manager app for Pinterest/developer review and connected content workflows.

Privacy PolicyTermsReturn Home
Purpose

Content management

The app is intended to help Digital Asset Vault GB create, organise and review Pinterest content for its own products.

Use

Internal business account

The app is for Digital Asset Vault GB’s own Pinterest business workflow, including boards, pins and performance review where authorised.

Data

Limited access

Only data permitted by approved API scopes is accessed, such as account details, boards, pins and reporting for the connected account.

Controls

Revocable access

Access can be revoked through the connected platform account settings at any time.
